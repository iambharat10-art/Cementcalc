Thermodynamic Formulas

1. Normal Air Density

ρₙ = P / (R × T)
where P = 101,325 Pa, R = 286.9 J/(kg·K), T = 293.15 K (20°C)
ρₙ ≈ 1.2047 kg/Nm³

2. Mass Flow Rate

ṁ = Q × ρₙ (kg/h) → ÷ 3600 → kg/s

3. Compression Ratio

r = P_out / P_in
where P_out = P_out_gauge + P_atm, P_in = P_in_gauge + P_atm

4. Compressor Power (Isentropic)

W = ṁ × Cp × T₁ / ηc × (r^((γ-1)/γ) - 1)
where Cp ≈ 1.005 kJ/(kg·K) for air, T₁ in Kelvin

5. Discharge Temperature

Isentropic: T_disch_s = T_in × (P_out/P_in)^((γ-1)/γ)
Real: T_disch = T_in + (T_disch_s - T_in) / ηc

6. Motor Power

Motor = W × 1.05 (5% auxiliary margin)
