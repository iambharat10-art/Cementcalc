CEMA 7th Edition — Standard Tables & Formulas
Cross-Section Formulas
Edge Clearance
c = 0.055 × b + 2.286 (cm)
3-Roll Equal Troughed Geometry
b' = b − 2c [active width]
L = CEMA std table or ≈ 0.455 × b' [center roll length]
m = (b'−L)/2 [wing slant length]
I = m·sin(β) , f = m·cos(β)
L₁ = L + 2f [surcharge chord]
Areas & Capacity
A₁ = (L+L₁)/2 × I [trapezoid]
r = (L₁/2)/sin(α) [arc radius]
A₂ = r²/2×(2α−sin2α) [segment]
Q = A×V×ρ×3600 [tph, A in m²]
Q_eff = Q × fill%/100
Power & Motor Formulas
IS 11592 — Main Friction
F_H = f·C·L·g·(mRU+mRO+mB·cosδ+mG·cosδ)
f=0.020 standard
Gradient & Total
F_St = (mG+mB)·g·H
P_eff = F_total × V / 1000 [kW]
P_motor = P_eff / η × 1.1 [η=0.95]
